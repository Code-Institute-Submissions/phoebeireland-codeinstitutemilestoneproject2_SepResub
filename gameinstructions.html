Idle Square Factory

Welcome to Idle Square Factory!


How to Play:

  • Click the blue square to earn money
  • Use the money earned to buy upgrades
  • Upgrades unlock at certain money threshholds
  • The First upgrade unlocks at $20, the Second at $40 and so on
  • Upgrades will help you earn more money
  • To save your score before you leave the page, press the "Save Score" button at the bottom of the page
  • Make as much money as you can to become the most profitable Square Factory!
